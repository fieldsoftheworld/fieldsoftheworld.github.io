FTW is an open, community-driven initiative to advance global agricultural intelligence. We welcome contributions from researchers, developers, organizations, and anyone passionate about improving agricultural monitoring through technology.

Ways to Contribute

We welcome contributions of:

  • Field boundary datasets - Help expand our global coverage
  • Model improvements - Advance the state-of-the-art
  • Tool development - Build better workflows and interfaces
  • Promotion and educational materials - Blog posts, documentation, talks, papers, social media
  • Feedback and ideas - Share your experiences and suggestions
